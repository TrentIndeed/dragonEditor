# Platform-Specific Editing Rules

Systematic rules derived from research for the AI pipeline to follow per platform.

---

## TikTok

### Pacing
- **Cut frequency**: 1-3 second cuts as default pacing
- **Opening**: 0.8-1.5 second cuts in first 5 seconds
- **Max static shot**: 5 seconds before a visual change (zoom, B-roll, text)
- **Optimal length**: 21-34 seconds for max completion, 31-60 seconds for watch time
- **Completion rate target**: 70%+ to trigger algorithmic distribution

### Hook (first 3 seconds)
- Second 0-1: Visual interrupt (zoom + motion)
- Second 1-2: Auditory hook + text overlay
- Second 2-3: Verbal promise resolving the visual tease
- **71% of viewers decide in 3 seconds; average decision at 1.7 seconds**

### Captions
- Style: Word-by-word highlight animation ("CapCut karaoke")
- Font: Montserrat Bold (business), Poppins (lifestyle), TikTok Sans (native)
- Size: 48-62px at 1080x1920
- Color: White text + black stroke; yellow/green keyword highlights
- Position: Center-middle, avoid bottom 480px and top 15%
- Chunks: 3-7 words, 1-3 seconds per chunk
- **80% more likely to finish video with captions**

### Zooms
- 1.2x-1.5x punch-in at vocal emphasis, punchlines, data points
- NOT at fixed intervals (feels robotic)
- Slow zoom (100% to 110-120% over 3-10s) for tension building
- For ages 13-24: visual change every 15-25 seconds

### B-Roll
- 50-80% B-roll, 20-50% talking head
- B-roll clips: 3-5 seconds each
- Never leave talking head on screen >5 seconds without visual change

### Audio
- Background music mandatory (98% more views)
- Sound effects every 5-15 seconds
- SFX at -10 to -20dB, dialogue at -6 to -12dB, music at -18 to -22dB
- Use trending sounds early in their cycle
- 0.3 second strategic pauses at natural breathing points

### Safe Zones
- Bottom 480px: TikTok UI (captions, buttons)
- Keep critical content in center 60% of frame

## Instagram Reels

### Pacing
- **Optimal length**: 7-15 seconds for viral loops
- **Loop strategy**: Short loops that play 3x generate 300% retention signal
- Slightly more polished/aesthetic than TikTok
- Faster scrollers seeking "vibes" over information

### Hook
- Aesthetic/vibe-driven intros
- Visual quality matters more than on TikTok
- 22% more engagement than regular video posts

### Captions
- Same word-by-word style but trending toward minimalist
- Clean sans-serif fonts, white text with subtle shadows
- Avoid bottom 320px and top 250px

### Zooms & B-Roll
- Same 1.2-1.5x punch-in rules as TikTok
- More cinematic B-roll preferred (not meme-heavy)
- Smooth fades emerging over hard cuts for lifestyle content

### Audio
- Same levels as TikTok
- Music selection: slightly more polished/curated feel

### Safe Zones
- Bottom 320px, top 250px for UI elements
- Center 60% for critical text

---

## YouTube Shorts

### Pacing
- **Cut frequency**: 2-4 seconds between cuts
- **Optimal length**: 30-60 seconds (discovery sweet spot)
- "Viewed vs. swiped" ratio: target 50%+
- Search-driven evergreen discoverability (can go viral weeks later)

### Hook
- Keyword-first, search-optimized openings
- Front-load the topic in first 3 seconds
- YouTube SEO matters more than trend-riding

### Captions
- Same styling as TikTok/Reels
- Avoid bottom 270px, right 190px for UI buttons
- Keywords in first 3 seconds of on-screen text

### Zooms & B-Roll
- Same rules as TikTok
- Slightly longer holds acceptable (audience skews older)

### Audio
- Same mixing levels
- Less trend-dependent; evergreen music preferred
- CPM: $0.01-$0.07 per thousand views (much lower than long-form)

### Safe Zones
- Bottom 270px, right 190px

## YouTube Long-Form

### Pacing
- **Average shot length**: 4-7 seconds baseline
- **Visual change every**: 15-25 seconds
- **Attention reset every**: 1-2 minutes with pacing shift
- **Re-hook every**: 30-60 seconds ("Later I'll show you...")
- **MrBeast benchmark**: ~23 cuts per 60 seconds, ~70% average retention

### Structure
- "Staircase storytelling" — start small, escalate progressively
- End abruptly on a high note — never signal the video is ending
- Chapters every 2-5 minutes with keyword-optimized titles
- Mid-roll ads: 1 at 50-60% mark for 8-12 min, every 5-7 min for 20+ min

### Hook
- CTR x Average View Duration = algorithmic promotion
- Thumbnail: single focal point, face with emotion, 3-5 bold words, high contrast
- Title: front-load keywords in first 40 chars, under 60 total
- Thumbnail + title = two halves of one puzzle (never repeat info)

### Zooms
- For ages 25+: maintain shots 20-40 seconds, simpler transitions
- Burst editing pattern: steady 15-25s pacing, then 5-10 rapid cuts every 2-3 min

### B-Roll
- 40-50% for tutorials, 20-30% for podcasts
- B-roll clips: 5-8 seconds (longer than short-form)
- Images/screenshots: 2-4 seconds with entrance animation

### Audio
- Music at -18 to -22dB, dropping to -25dB during calm, rising to -8dB during energy
- Strategic silence before major reveals
- Change tracks when story shifts
- Beat-synced editing for energetic sections

### Thumbnail Rules
- Single focal point (cluttered = -23% CTR)
- Exaggerated facial emotion (+20-30% CTR)
- Max 3-5 bold words readable at phone size
- 30%+ brightness differential for contrast
